# Game Plan – "Collateral Damage"

## Core Idea
The player takes on the role of a cleanup crew worker in a world where a powerful hero constantly battles enemies. Instead of being the hero, the player must deal with the aftermath by cleaning debris, repairing damage, and avoiding hazards caused by the hero’s actions.

## Core Gameplay Loop
Enter area → Clean debris → Repair damage → Avoid hazards → Gain points → Repeat

## Mechanics

### Player Systems
- Movement (CharacterController)
- Interaction system (cleaning & repairing)

### Gameplay Systems
- Debris pickup system
- Repair system (fix broken objects)
- Hazard system (fire, explosions, dangerous zones)
- Score/progress system

### Optional Features (Stretch Goals)
- Combo system for efficient cleaning
- Time pressure mechanic
- Random hazard spawning

## AI Tools Used

### Coding
- Cursor → Generate gameplay scripts
- ChatGPT → Debugging, refining logic, explaining code

### Art
- Leonardo AI → Generate textures and UI elements

### Sound
- ElevenLabs / Soundraw → Generate sound effects and voice lines

### Writing
- ChatGPT → Documentation, dialogue, planning

## Development Plan

### Day 1 – Ideation & Setup
- Finalise game concept
- Create documentation (plan.md, readme.md)

- Setup Git repository

### Day 2 – Prototype Build
- Implement player movement

### Day 3 – Refinement & Polish
- Improve UI (score/progress display)

## Reflection Prompts 
- Where did AI excel?
    - AI truly excelled in allowing for a smooth game development, especially in terms of generating scripts as well as applying them and also guiding me through the entire game development process and the project as a whole.
- Where did AI fail or mislead?
    - AI mainly failed and misled in sound generation as it often would not produce the sound I was describing. I was misled in image generation a bit as it took multiple attempts at prompts and ongoing descriptions to get the images I desired.
- How did AI affect my workflow?
    - AI sped up my workflow as I got a lot done more quickly and I believe to a good quality, but in certain areas AI did stall me as I reached certain limitations of different AIs such as the image generation and sound generation. 124
- Ethical considerations of AI usage
    - I feel that I used AI respectfully in terms of originality, transparency and fair use.
- What I would change next time? 
    - I would colloborate with AI to guide me again and generate layouts for my coding scripts but not do the entire coding as I would like to attempt them myself in order to personalize the game and also be able to learn and grow my skills.
- Did I credit appropriately?
    - I believe that I credited appropriately.
- Did relying on AI feel ethically questionable?
    - There were certain moments where relying on AI did feel ethically questionable, such as having the AI generate my full scripts for my game as then I feel I myself contributed less towards the project.
- Future authentic and responsible use of AI?
    - I will ensure to credit all the AI use fairly and appropriately and make sure to use AI originally, transparently and fairly.
